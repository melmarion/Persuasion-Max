## RULE VIOLATIONS: DO NOT USE

### üî¥ RULE 1 VIOLATIONS: False Claims

**Fabricated Authority:**
- Synthetic expertise (deepfakes, AI-generated credentials)
- Misrepresented qualifications or credentials
- Creates false impression of professional endorsement

**Misrepresented Identity:**
- Commercial actors presented as independent users
- Undisclosed paid endorsements
- Organized campaigns presented as organic

**Obscured Commercial Context:**
- Sponsored content without clear advertising disclosure
- Undisclosed business relationships
- Commercial motivation not apparent to audience

**Inaccurate Consensus Signals:**
- Fabricated popularity indicators
- Artificial amplification of minority perspectives
- Coordinated inauthentic behavior appearing organic

---

### üü† RULE 2 VIOLATIONS: Obstruction of Exit

**Exit Friction Patterns:**
- Cancellation procedures more complex than signup
- Multiple confirmation screens blocking exit
- Unsubscribe mechanisms intentionally obscured

**Delayed Account Deletion:**
- Extended deletion timelines allowing account restoration
- Persistent data retention after user requests deletion
- Deletion processes requiring repeated confirmation

**Obscured Opt-Out Options:**
- Opt-out mechanisms difficult to locate or access
- Require account authentication to opt-out
- Technical barriers to exercising opt-out choice

---

### üü° RULE 3 VIOLATIONS: Crisis Targeting

**Vulnerable Population Targeting:**
- Marketing addictive products to susceptible demographics
- Financial products targeting those in economic distress
- Psychologically targeted content to vulnerable individuals
- Age-inappropriate engagement design for minors

**Identity Entrenchment Through Social Mechanisms:**
- Public position reinforcement preventing reversal
- Social cost creating barriers to belief change
- Tribal belonging structures preventing exit
- Amplification locking individuals into positions

**Extreme Content Escalation:**
- Systems progressively introducing more extreme content
- Algorithmic identification and targeting of susceptible users
- Systematic funneling toward fringe perspectives
- Coordinated escalation of ideological positioning
