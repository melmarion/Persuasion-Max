## WHAT EACH FRAMEWORK DOES

### STIMULI DETECTION (6 Total)
Each stimulus can be measured 0-100 independently, then combined for composite score.

#### STIMULUS 1: PERSONAL (Self-Centered Targeting)
**Detection Rules:**
- Exclusion language: "Not for everyone" = 20 pts
- Status threat: "Fear of being basic" = 30 pts each
- Tribal safety signals: "You understand the references" = 25 pts each

**Real Example:** Luxury fashion copy "If you know, you know" = 65/100
**Regex:** `(?i)(not\s+for\s+everyone|for\s+those\s+who|if\s+you\s+know)`

#### STIMULUS 2: CONTRASTABLE (Binary Framing)
**Detection Rules:**
- Clear before/after or A/B contrast
- Ideological binary framing
- Spectrum rejection

**Real Example:** "Commercial vs. Archive" framing = 70/100
**Regex:** `(?i)(while.*they|we.*you|us.*them)`

#### STIMULUS 3: TANGIBLE (Concrete Specificity)
**Detection Rules:**
- Exact measurements: "680gsm cotton" = 20 pts
- Material specificity beats generics
- "Made in [specific defunct town]" = 25 pts

**Real Example:** "Shrinks 2% after first wash" = 55/100
**Pattern:** Numeric measurements + material descriptors

#### STIMULUS 4: MEMORABLE (U-Shaped Retention)
**Detection Rules:**
- First encounter: Grabber (high impact)
- Last encounter: Close (unresolved hook)
- Middle: Sparse/minimal

**Real Example:** Apple "one more thing" = 80/100
**Pattern:** Opening visceral image + ending question

#### STIMULUS 5: VISUAL (Anti-Aesthetic Design)
**Detection Rules:**
- Mistakes = authenticity proof: grain, blur, bad lighting
- Surveillance aesthetics: CCTV quality
- Documentary coldness: no styling

**Real Example:** Luxury brand Instagram = 70/100
**Pattern:** Accidentally-shot aesthetic that looks intentional

#### STIMULUS 6: EMOTIONAL (Fear-to-Relief Arc)
**Detection Rules:**
- Fear activation: "You're still too visible" = 30 pts
- Relief trigger: Tribal recognition
- Emotional ambivalence: "You're in. Or you were already."

**Real Example:** Luxury brand copy = 75/100
**Pattern:** Melancholy + discovery feeling
